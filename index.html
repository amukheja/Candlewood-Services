About Us

Experienced operators.
Exceptional results.

Candlewood Services LLC is a Cary, NC–based construction and remodeling firm built on a foundation of real estate investment expertise. Our leadership team has participated in over $18 billion of real estate transactions across 25 years — bringing institutional-grade discipline to residential and commercial projects of all sizes.

Deep Triangle market knowledge — We operate across Durham, Cary, Raleigh, and the broader Research Triangle, with an intimate understanding of local market dynamics, permit processes, and subcontractor networks.
Investment-minded execution — Every decision is evaluated for its impact on project ROI, timeline, and asset quality. We think like owners because we are owners.
Transparent, direct communication — Clients receive clear scopes, honest budgets, and regular project updates. No surprises.
