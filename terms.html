1. Program Description

Candlewood Services LLC operates an SMS messaging program for business-to-business project coordination and client support. Messages are sent to contractors, vendors, subcontractors, and business clients for the purpose of coordinating construction and renovation project activities, including job assignments, scope of work delivery, access instructions, material orders, and project support communications.

Program Name: Candlewood Services Project Coordination & Support

2. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on active project activity. Recipients may receive multiple messages per day during active project phases, or infrequent messages when no active project work is ongoing. Message frequency is determined by business need and is not a fixed recurring schedule.

3. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS and data rates charged by your mobile carrier apply to all messages sent and received through this program. Candlewood Services does not charge recipients any fee for receiving messages.

4. How to Opt In

Recipients are enrolled in this messaging program by providing their mobile phone number to Candlewood Services in connection with a business engagement — such as during contractor onboarding, project setup, or vendor coordination — and acknowledging that they may receive project-related SMS communications. Recipients may also opt in by texting START to our business number.

5. How to Opt Out (STOP)

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from Candlewood Services. Upon receipt of your STOP request, we will send one final confirmation message and will not send further messages to your number unless you re-enroll.
