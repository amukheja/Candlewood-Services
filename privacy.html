SMS / Text Messaging
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2025  ·  Candlewood Services LLC

1. Overview

Candlewood Services LLC ("Candlewood Services," "we," "us," or "our") operates a business-to-business (B2B) SMS communication program to coordinate project work with contractors, vendors, subcontractors, and other professional contacts (collectively, "Recipients"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our SMS messaging program.

2. Information We Collect

When you engage with Candlewood Services via SMS, we may collect the following information:

  • Mobile phone number provided directly to us during the contracting or onboarding process
  • Name and business contact information provided in connection with a project
  • Message content and communication history related to project coordination
  • Opt-in and opt-out records for compliance purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for legitimate business communication purposes, including:

  • Coordinating project schedules, scopes of work, and job assignments
  • Communicating job site access information and instructions
  • Sending and confirming material orders and procurement requests
  • Providing project status updates and follow-up support
  • Maintaining records of consent and opt-out requests

4. How We Share Your Information

Candlewood Services does not sell, rent, trade, or share your mobile phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share your information with data brokers or advertising networks.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in delivering SMS messages (e.g., telecommunications carriers and messaging platforms) strictly for the purpose of message delivery
  • As required by law, legal process, or government authority
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition, with appropriate protections in place

5. Data Retention

We retain contact information and message records only as long as necessary to support active business relationships and comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements. When information is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

6. Your Rights and Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from Candlewood Services at any time by replying STOP to any message. Upon receipt of a STOP request, we will cease sending messages to that number promptly.

To request deletion of your contact information from our records, please contact us directly at the information provided below.

7. Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your contact information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our SMS communications relate exclusively to professional project coordination and do not involve the transmission of sensitive financial or personal identification data.

8. No Marketing Use

Our SMS program is strictly a business operations and project support channel. It is not used for marketing, promotions, or solicitation of any kind.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our SMS practices, please contact:

Candlewood Services LLC
Cary, North Carolina

This Privacy Policy applies solely to SMS/text message communications sent by Candlewood Services LLC. It does not govern other data practices, which may be covered by a separate general privacy policy.
